STORAGE FACILITIES

Reliable storage facilities

Silos

storage structures typically used for storing grain, seeds, or other bulk commodities,designed to keep the stored products dry and protect them from pests and other contaminants.

Warehouse

large buildings used for storing a variety of agricultural products, such as grains, fruits, and vegetables,equipped with loading docks and other features for easy transportation.

Cold Storage

designed to store perishable agricultural products, such as fruits and vegetables, at low temperatures to extend their shelf life. These facilities are refrigerated or frozen and can be used for short-term or long-term storage.

Root Cellars

underground storage structures used for storing root vegetables, such as potatoes, carrots, and onions, typically cool and humid, which helps to maintain the freshness of the stored products.

Bins and Bags

commonly used for storing smaller quantities of agricultural products, such as nuts, seeds, and dried fruits.

Drying and Curing

for preparing harvested crops for storage, designed to remove excess moisture from the products, which helps to prevent spoilage and extend their shelf life.
